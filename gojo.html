Intro

Who's this guy?

Satoru Gojo is a character in the anime Jujutsu Kaisen, most commonly known for his charm and
his status as the strongest of jujutsu sorcerers. Satoru is a special grade sorcerer, and became one alongside Suguru Geto and Shoko Ieri.

Gojo's past/about him

Satoru is a member of the Gojo clan, with abilities like the Limitless technique, which manipulates
space at an atomic level, and the Six Eyes, which grant him microscopic perception and flawless cursed energy.
Masamichi Yaga was gojo's primary instructor during Satoru's time at Jujutsu High, and Masamichi taught him along with Suguru and Shoko.
